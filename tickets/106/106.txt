Child headcount (attendance check) for "My Group"

Implement a child headcount (attendance check) feature for children in "My Group" only (the group of the currently logged-in user).

Placement:
The feature should be easily accessible, with as few clicks as possible. Two candidate locations:
- https://sre-nrw.de/group-edit (most likely, as it is closely related to group management), or
- https://sre-nrw.de/main (if faster access is preferred).
The final placement can be decided during implementation.

Functionality:
Add a "Headcount" (or similar) button that opens a list of all children in the current user's group.

For each child, display two attendance indicators:

1. Present in the morning
   - Determined automatically based on whether the child arrived by bus.
   - Read-only.
   - Remains unchanged throughout the day.

2. Present now
   - Can be updated multiple times during the day during a headcount.
   - Staff can mark whether the child is currently present.
   - It should be immediately obvious when a child is marked as absent.

When a user changes the "Present now" status, the update should be sent to the database immediately. There is no need for an additional Save or Submit button.

The feature only applies to children who belong to the user's own group.
